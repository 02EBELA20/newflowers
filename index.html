FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How long does delivery take? +

We offer delivery within 24 hours of your order placement, ensuring your flowers arrive fresh and on time. For urgent requests, we can often accommodate same-day delivery—please contact us to confirm availability!

Can I customize my bouquet? +

Absolutely! We pride ourselves on maximum flexibility to meet your needs. You can customize your bouquet by selecting specific flowers, colors, and arrangements. Contact us, and we’ll tailor your order to your exact preferences.

How do I care for my flowers? +

To keep your flowers fresh, trim the stems at an angle every few days, change the water regularly, and keep them away from direct sunlight or heat. We also provide care instructions with every order to ensure your bouquet lasts as long as possible.

Do you offer same-day delivery? +

Yes, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2 PM, subject to availability. Our 24-hour delivery service ensures your flowers are delivered promptly, even for last-minute requests.

How do I place an order? +

You can place your order by contacting us via WhatsApp or through our social media channels listed on the website. We’ll guide you through the process to ensure your order is perfect!
