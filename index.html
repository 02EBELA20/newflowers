Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

+

Delivery typically takes 1-2 business days within the city, and 3-5 days for international orders, depending on your location.

Can I customize my bouquet?

+

Yes! We offer customization options. Please contact us or add a note during checkout with your preferences.

How do I care for my flowers?

+

Keep your flowers in a cool place, away from direct sunlight. Change the water every two days and trim the stems slightly to help them last longer.

Do you offer same-day delivery?

+

Yes, we offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 2 PM in your local time zone. Additional fees may apply.
